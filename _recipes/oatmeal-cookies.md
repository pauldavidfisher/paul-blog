Oatmeal cookies are a proverbial favorite with both kids and adults. This crisp and chewy cookie is loaded with oats, dried fruit, and chopped nuts.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter, softened
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 3 cups quick cooking oats

## Directions

1. In a medium bowl, cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
2. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
3. Mix in oats.
4. Cover, and chill dough for at least one hour.
5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
6. Flatten each cookie with a large fork dipped in sugar.
7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in preheated oven. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
