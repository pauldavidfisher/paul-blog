The day started with birthday wishes flooding in for Chloe—or "Stoey," "Coco," "Coco Monkey," depending on who you ask. Tyler kicked things off bright and early, followed by Sophia's message overflowing with heart emojis and declarations of love. Chrisje and Paul chimed in too, with Paul surprising Chloe with a gift delivered straight to her. Her excited "OMG I'm so excited" said it all. Plans were made for a video call once Tyler finished his class that evening.

Woven through the birthday joy was news about Grandpa Robert. The family had been praying since the night before, checking in for updates throughout the day. By evening, Sophia shared the good news everyone had been hoping for: he's doing remarkably well. Not out of the woods yet, she reminded everyone, but things were looking up. Tyler and Chrisje confirmed they'd keep praying—it's what this family does.

In between the big moments, conversation bounced around in true Fisher fashion—debating whether that thing in a photo was a stick bug or a cricket, trading hot takes about Rhode Island's chaotic driving (East Providence versus the East Side of Providence is apparently a crucial distinction), and reminiscing about a favorite spot they all used to visit together. Sophia marveled at photos from the Netherlands that Tyler had taken, barely believing the beauty was real. Just another day of love, laughter, and staying connected across the miles.
